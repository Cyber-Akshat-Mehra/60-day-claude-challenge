**Spiritual practices:** Given the Moon-Saturn conjunction, a grounding daily practice (even 10 minutes of quiet reflection or journaling) is traditionally recommended more than anything elaborate — it works with your temperament rather than against it.

**Gemstones — use caution:**
- **Blue Sapphire (Neelam)** for Saturn is classically indicated given its yogakaraka status *and* friendly-sign placement — but Blue Sapphire is a high-risk stone that can backfire badly if it doesn't suit you personally. **Don't wear it without a supervised trial period** (a qualified astrologer/gemologist testing it on you for several days first).
- **Emerald (Panna)** for Mercury (a genuine functional benefit, not combust) is a gentler, lower-risk option if you want to explore gemstone support at all.
- **Avoid Yellow Sapphire (Pukhraj)** despite the Jupiter Mahadasha — Jupiter is functionally malefic for Libra Lagna (rules your 3rd and 6th houses), so strengthening it via gemstone could amplify friction/competition themes rather than ease them. This is a common mistake people make ("wear a gem for whichever dasha is running") — it doesn't apply cleanly here.
