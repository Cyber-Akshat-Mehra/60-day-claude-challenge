Nutrition data sources

Where the underlying composition figures are generally drawn from.

USDA FoodData Central

Baseline reference for macro and micronutrient values on common international foods.

Indian Food Composition Tables (IFCT 2017)

National Institute of Nutrition dataset used to approximate Indian staples and regional dishes.

ICMR-NIN Dietary Guidelines for Indians

Reference point for macro distribution and activity-based energy multipliers.

WHO / FAO nutrient intake guidance

General basis for micronutrient RDAs such as iron, calcium, zinc and folate targets.

Figures used in this app are rounded, generalised averages per 100g and are illustrative — they are not a substitute for laboratory food testing or a clinical nutrition assessment.

NutriScope estimates are approximate and for informational purposes only — not medical advice. Consult a registered dietitian or physician for personalised guidance, particularly around diagnosed conditions.
